Statutory insurance (ÖGK, BVAEB, SVS)

Treatments covered in full or in part by the Austrian Health Insurance Fund (ÖGK), BVAEB, SVS and other statutory insurance carriers – when prescribed by a doctor and a contract exists.

Massage therapies

Therapeutic massage is a manual therapy that affects muscles, tissue, vessels, internal organs and psyche. It promotes exchange between blood vessels and tissues, local blood flow, increase or decrease of muscle tone, pain relief and mental relaxation.

APM – Acupoint Meridian Massage

Acupuncture without needles

Acupoint Meridian Massage is an energy-based treatment combining Western and Eastern approaches. There are around 360 acupuncture points on meridians (energy pathways) that work like switches. While acupuncture treats individual points with needles, APM works without needles and is non-invasive.

Causes:

Healed fractures Post-operative scars Dietary habits Climate factors Social environment Spine and joint posture Emotional or psychological aspects

Applications:

Headaches Migraine Neuralgia Rheumatic complaints Spine complaints Sciatica Tinnitus Vertigo Shoulder-arm syndrome Low back pain Constipation

Goal:

The goal of APM is to restore balance to your energy flow and activate your body's own regulation and self-healing.
