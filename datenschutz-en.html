Privacy Policy

Information on how we handle your data in accordance with the GDPR.

1. Data controller

Responsible for data processing on this website:

Dr. med. univ. Fadime Cenik
Kaiser-Ebersdorfer-Straße 328, 1110 Vienna, Austria
Phone: 01 / 769 29 91
Email: office@drcenik.at

2. General / Legal basis

Personal data are processed only to the extent necessary, lawfully and in a transparent manner. Legal bases include: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), and where applicable legal obligations (e.g. retention requirements).

3. Contact and appointment form

When you use the contact or appointment form, the data you provide (name, email, optionally phone, message) are processed solely to handle your enquiry or arrange an appointment. Transmission is via FormSubmit (FormSubmit LLC) over an encrypted connection to office@drcenik.at. Legal basis: your consent (checkbox before submitting) and pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(a) and (b) GDPR). Data are not passed on to third parties unless required by law.

4. Google Maps

On the contact page, maps from Google Maps (Google Ireland Limited, Ireland) may be embedded. The map is loaded only after your explicit consent (“Accept all” in the cookie notice or “Load map” button). When loaded, data (including IP address and device information) are transmitted to Google. For more information see Google’s Privacy Policy. Legal basis with consent: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

5. Cookies and local storage

We store your cookie preference (drcenik-cookie-consent) and optionally form inputs temporarily in the browser (localStorage) so nothing is lost if you close the page by mistake. This data does not leave your browser and is deleted after successful submission or when the form is reset. Legal basis: technical necessity or your consent (Art. 6(1)(f) or (a) GDPR). We do not use analytics or marketing cookies.

6. Analytics / Tracking

We currently do not use any analytics or tracking tools (e.g. Google Analytics) on this website. If this changes in the future, we will inform you and offer consent or opt-out options.

7. Hosting / Server log data

When you visit our website, the hosting provider or server automatically collects access data (e.g. IP address, date/time, page accessed, browser type). These are stored only to the extent and for the period necessary for operation and security (legitimate interest / legal obligation where applicable).

8. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access (Art. 15 GDPR), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20), and to object (Art. 21), as well as the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Austria: Austrian Data Protection Authority, Barichgasse 40–42, 1030 Vienna, dsb.gv.at). To exercise your rights, please contact the data controller listed above.

9. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. The current version is available on this page.
