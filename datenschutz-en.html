Privacy Policy

Information on how we handle your data in accordance with the GDPR.

2. General / Legal basis

Personal data are processed only to the extent necessary, lawfully and in a transparent manner. Legal bases include: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), and where applicable legal obligations (e.g. retention requirements).

3. Contact form

When you use the contact form, the data you provide (name, email, optionally phone, message) are processed solely to handle your enquiry or to arrange an appointment. Processing is based on your consent (by submitting the form after having read and acknowledged the privacy notice) and for pre-contractual measures. Data are not passed on to third parties unless required by law. Data are deleted after your request has been dealt with or after any retention period has expired, unless you have given explicit consent to longer storage.

4. Google Maps

Our website embeds maps from Google Maps (Google Ireland Limited, Ireland). When you open pages that contain an embedded map, data (including your IP address and device information) are transmitted to Google. Google may use these data for personalised advertising and to create usage profiles. For more information see Google’s Privacy Policy. The embedding is for the purpose of displaying our location (legitimate interest). You can avoid the map function by not opening the relevant page or by blocking Google content in your browser.

5. Cookies and local storage

We use technically necessary or functional cookies and local storage (e.g. to store your cookie consent preference). These are required for the operation of the website or reflect your explicit choice (e.g. “Accept all” in the cookie notice). We do not use analytics or marketing cookies without your consent. You can set your browser to notify you when cookies are set or to reject them; some website functions may then be limited.

6. Analytics / Tracking

We currently do not use any analytics or tracking tools (e.g. Google Analytics) on this website. If this changes in the future, we will inform you and offer consent or opt-out options.

7. Hosting / Server log data

When you visit our website, the hosting provider or server automatically collects access data (e.g. IP address, date/time, page accessed, browser type). These are stored only to the extent and for the period necessary for operation and security (legitimate interest / legal obligation where applicable).

8. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access (Art. 15 GDPR), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20), and to object (Art. 21), as well as the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Austria: Austrian Data Protection Authority, Barichgasse 40–42, 1030 Vienna, dsb.gv.at). To exercise your rights, please contact the data controller listed above.

9. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. The current version is available on this page.
